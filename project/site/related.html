Links to other Scouting Sites

Summary

There are many sites that offer help to all scouting disciplines. Many scouts find help on the web and here we endevour to provide our scouts with safe, verified links. The scouting program does incorporate internet safety into their materials for training both scouts and parents. All of our troop promotes and supports internet safety. For questions or concerns please contact us and we can direct you to many resources to help you.

Links

BSA National Council
The National Scout Office. On this site you can find links to policies that the scouts follow as well as volunteer resources. Many questions can be answered on this site.
National Online Portal
The National Online Portal allows you to check your registration status and current BSA information. In addition to this the portal allows you to stay up to date on your training including Youth Protection Training.
Merit Badge Dot ORG
Merit Badge Dot ORG offers many advancement resources for scouts. From descriptions of Merit Badges and Worksheets to accompany the learning the Merit Badges provide.
Northern Star Council
Northern Star Council is the local governing body that supports our troop. They provide camps, training, recruiting, and information for all scouting programs. They also run the local scout shops.
United States Scouting Service Project
The United States Scouting Service Project hosts not only the advancement resources found on Merit Badge Dot ORG but also provides position descriptions, skits, songs and Scout Master's Minuets. All kinds of other resources can be found on this site and we encourage you to check it out.
National Scout Shop
ScoutStuff.org provides you with an online outlet to purchase all your official scout gear. There are catalogs for the BSA and 37N outfitters (The Bechtel Summit Reserve store) as well as special catalogs for the World Scout Jamboree and National Jamborees when available.
National Order of the Arrow
The Order of the Arrow is the BSA's service organization. It honors the scouts that show cheerful service to their fellow scouters. The brotherhood is a strong bond between its members. Resources for Arrowmen can be found here.
National Eagle Scout Association
For all the Eagle Alumni of our troop, keep in touch with your fellow brethren. The National Eagle Scout Association provides a forum to keep in touch with Eagle Scouts around the globe. Join this proud organization today.
