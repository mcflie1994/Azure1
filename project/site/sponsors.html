Troop 101 Sponsors Biographies
Monticello American Legion
The Monticello American Legion is the chartering organization for our troop. They sponsor us with our council and provide us with a community tie. With them we participate in many community events including parades and other events.
Monticello United Methodist Church
The Monticello United Methodist Church is a supporting organization of our scout troop. They let us use their fellowship hall for our Monday meetings. The church also lets us use their grounds for many outdoor based activities.
